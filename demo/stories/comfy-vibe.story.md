# Comfy Vibe — Workspace App Story

## Who
A ComfyUI user — developer, artist, or team lead — who wants to organize their workflows, share projects with collaborators, and access templates and tutorials from a single workspace.

## Goal
Tour the Comfy Vibe workspace app, demonstrating how it organizes projects, templates, shared assets, and provides search/sort/filter capabilities.

## Happy path

### Chapter 1 — Sidebar navigation
1. **My Workspace button** — The top-level home button that brings everything into one view.
2. **OVERVIEW section** — Recents (recently opened files), Templates (pre-built starting points), Tutorials (guided walkthroughs).
3. **PRIVATE section** — Personal projects only you can access: My First Project, Personal Work.
4. **SHARED section** — Collaborative projects visible to invited teammates: Client Project, Portrait Workflow, Custom LoRA, Reference Images.
5. **Utility links** — Trash (recoverable deleted items) and Help (documentation).

### Chapter 2 — Header workflow thumbnails
6. **Simple txt2img** — The classic text-to-image starting workflow.
7. **Upscale Pipeline** — Resolution enhancement workflow.
8. **Multi-Pass Refiner** — Multiple sampling passes for quality.
9. **Minimal** — Stripped-down bare-essentials workflow.

### Chapter 3 — Search, sort, and filter
10. **Search bar** — Filter everything by keyword.
11. **Sort dropdown** — Last Updated, Name, or Type.
12. **Filter tabs** — Recent, Shared with me, Favorites.

### Chapter 4 — Getting started
13. **Empty state** — "No recent files" placeholder.
14. **Create your first workflow** — Call-to-action button to begin.

## Key features to demo
- Full sidebar navigation with organized sections
- Workflow thumbnail cards in header
- Search, sort, and filter controls
- Empty state with clear CTA
- Private vs Shared project separation

## Out of scope
- Actually creating or editing a workflow (requires canvas)
- Login or authentication flows
- Settings or preferences
