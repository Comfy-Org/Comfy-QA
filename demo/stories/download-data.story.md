# ComfyUI Download Data — User Story

## Who
A ComfyUI ecosystem stakeholder — a custom-node author gauging adoption, a content creator deciding whether to cover ComfyUI, or a contributor checking growth momentum.

## Goal
Understand the download trajectory of ComfyUI across different time horizons and draw conclusions about ecosystem health and growth trends.

## Happy path

1. **Land on the dashboard** — They arrive via a link from Discord or the Comfy blog. The headline "ComfyUI Download Data" tells them exactly what they're looking at.
2. **Read the subtitle and change stats** — The subtitle reads "Daily New Downloads — net new downloads per day for all portable ComfyUI releases." Change statistics show something like +4,526 downloads/day and +77.6% growth over the selected timeframe.
3. **Inspect the chart** — A time-series chart dominates the page, showing download volume over time.
4. **Hover the chart** — Moving along the x-axis reveals per-day or per-period download counts via tooltips.
5. **Switch to 1 Week view** — They click "1 Week" to zoom into the most recent seven days, checking for daily patterns.
6. **Hover 1 Week data** — They explore the short-term data points, looking for weekday vs weekend patterns.
7. **Switch to 1 Month view** — They widen the window to see the last 30 days of activity.
8. **Hover 1 Month data** — They trace the month's trend line, looking for spikes or dips.
9. **Switch to 3 Months view** — A quarterly view reveals medium-term growth or stagnation.
10. **Hover 3 Months data** — They check whether the trend is accelerating or decelerating.
11. **Switch to All Time view** — The full history shows ComfyUI's entire growth arc.
12. **Hover All Time data** — They find key inflection points — launches, viral moments, major releases.
13. **Notice the GitHub link** — A link to the source repo shows this is an open-source community dashboard.
14. **Return to overview** — They scroll back to the top to take in the full picture one more time.

## Key features to demo
- Headline download metrics
- Interactive time-series chart
- Four time-range toggles (1 Week, 1 Month, 3 Months, All Time)
- Tooltip on hover for specific data points
- GitHub repository link
- Growth trend analysis across time horizons

## Implicit narration (what the voiceover should explain)
- WHY tracking downloads matters (ecosystem health, adoption signal)
- WHO benefits from this data (authors, investors, content creators)
- HOW to read the chart (x = time, y = downloads)
- WHAT the trends tell us about ComfyUI's trajectory
- HOW short-term vs long-term views reveal different insights

## Out of scope for this demo
- The underlying data pipeline or API
- Contributing to the dashboard codebase
- Comparing with other AI tool download stats
